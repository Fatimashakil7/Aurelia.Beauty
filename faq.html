Frequently Asked Questions

How do I chose the right product for my skin type?

Identify your skin type first_ Dry, Oily, Combination or Sensitive.Then chose products formulated specifically for your type.Check the ingredients list and avoid anything that triggers irritation.

What is the Skin Quiz?

Our Skin Quiz is a quick and personalized questionnaire that helps determine your skin type, concerns, and needs. Based on your answers, we recommend the most suitable product.

Are your products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, our products are formulated with gentle, dermatologist-tested ingredients. Always perform a patch test before full application.

How should I store skincare products?

Keep products in a cool, dry place ,away from direct sunlight. Close lids tightly to maintain freshness.

Can I return a product if I don't like it?

Yes! Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase. Products must be unused and in their original packaging.

Can I use multiple products together?

Yes, but introduce new products gradually Apply lighter textures first and avoid mixing strong active ingredients unless recommended.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Yes, all are products are cruelty-free and tested with ethical standards.
